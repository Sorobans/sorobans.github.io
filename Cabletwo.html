What is Cabletwo?

Cabletwo is many things; a place, a threat, a promise, but to put it simply:
Cabletwo is a bunch of people all streaming on the same twitch channel.
We like to try and focus on making things that are wacky and fun. Just look at Matrix Italiano, 500 Dogs, or Media Landscape for proof of that. But ANYONE can stream on Cabletwo! so long as you have a fun idea, you can join our channel and stream on it. It's fun! for more information on cabletwo, please go to Our EPIC neocities website :)!
